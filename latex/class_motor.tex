Since the \doxylink{class_p_i_d}{PID} controller expects an absolute error, the activation ratio is used to determine when to activate the controller. For example, with a motor turn of 90 degrees and an activation\+\_\+ratio of 0.\+3, the controller will output maximum voltage until the robot is within 90\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}0.3=27 degrees of the target angle.
